other calming resources

method 1 : talk about it

type out what you're feeling. if you want to talk to someone about it, hit submit and we'll email you back!


method 2 : exercise

  • one way to relax and de-stress is through breathing exercises. breathing exercises have a number of benefits and just the rhythm itself can be relaxing

  • another way to relax is through physical exercise, which can ease your body as well as your mind

method 3 : soothing sounds

listening to certain sounds can be very calming and soothing to the mind. try listening to the following sounds

  • ocean waves
  • rain and thunder
  • instrumental music
